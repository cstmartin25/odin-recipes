Avocado Toast

Simple guide for making delicious avocado toast at home!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2 slices Bread of choice
  • 'Everything but the bagel seasoning'
  • Siracha

Steps

  1. Toast Bread
  2. Spread avocado over the toast
  3. Drizzle siracha over toasts
  4. Sprinkle Everything but the bagel seasoning over toast and ENJOY!
