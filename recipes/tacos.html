Tacos

This guide will help you make delicious tacos within the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs. ground beef
  • Taco hard shells
  • mexican cheese 2 cups
  • Salsa
  • Taco Seasoning (1pack)
  • 2/3 cup water

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in skillet until browned
  2. Add water and taco seasoning packet, stir together
  3. Cook 4-5 minutes
  4. Empty contents into strainer over sink
  5. Serve into hard shells and garnish with cheese, salsa, etc and ENJOY!
