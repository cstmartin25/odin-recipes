Omellet Recipe

Description

Wisk eggs with youur veggies, add milk. Add the mixture to your heated pan and flip your omellet. cook to taste.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • milk
  • small tomatoes cut up
  • spinach
  • any other veggies, etc. :)

Steps

  1. Heat up skillet to medium heat
  2. Wisk eggs together with ingredients that you want in your omellet
  3. Pour that mixture into the pan
  4. cook 4-6 minuutres or until it looks like it can flip without coming apart
  5. After the flip, cook until it looks like it'll hold together / to desired level of cook-through
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
